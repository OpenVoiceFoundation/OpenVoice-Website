Distribution Strategy

Our distribution strategy is centered around making the assistive devices accessible to every individual in need. We aim to partner with local government units (LGUs), Special Education (SPED) schools, and communities to identify and support deaf individuals across the Philippines. By working closely with local organizations and government agencies, we will ensure that each device reaches the hands of those who need it the most.

We plan to distribute the devices for free to individuals, ensuring that cost is not a barrier to communication. Additionally, our focus will be on building a robust network of volunteers and contributors who can help us with the logistics of delivery and setup, especially in rural and remote areas.

Our distribution efforts will also include educational campaigns, where we will provide training and support for the users of our devices, ensuring they know how to maximize its potential. In this way, we aim to foster a culture of inclusivity where both deaf and hearing individuals can communicate effortlessly, enhancing mutual understanding and cooperation.

With your support, we will continue to expand our reach, ensuring that no one is left behind in our quest for accessible communication solutions for all.
